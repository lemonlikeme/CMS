Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our university

What are the admission requirements? +
To be eligible for admission, you need to have completed high school with a minimum GPA of 3.0, submit your transcripts, and provide two letters of recommendation. International students must also demonstrate English proficiency through TOEFL or IELTS scores.
How do I apply for financial aid? +
You can apply for financial aid by completing the FAFSA form and submitting it along with your application. We offer various scholarships, grants, and work-study programs. The financial aid office can help you explore all available options.
What housing options are available? +
We offer various housing options including traditional dormitories, apartment-style living, and themed housing communities. All first-year students are guaranteed housing, and upperclassmen can choose from multiple housing options based on availability.
How can I schedule a campus tour? +
You can schedule a campus tour through our website or by contacting the admissions office directly. We offer both guided tours and self-guided options, and you can also schedule meetings with faculty members or current students during your visit.
